Frequently asked
questions

What is your design philosophy?

At Ard INTERIOR, we believe in creating spaces that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional and sustainable. We take a holistic approach to design, considering the client's needs, lifestyle, and preferences to deliver tailored solutions that exceed their expectations.

How do you communicate with clients?

We maintain open and transparent communication with our clients throughout the design process. From regular project updates to timely responses to their queries, we ensure that our clients are informed and engaged every step of the way.

Do you offer warranty on your services?

Yes, we stand behind our work and offer a warranty on our design solutions and services. Our clients can be confident in the quality and durability of our work, and we are committed to addressing any concerns they may have post-project.

How long does an average interior design project take to complete?

The duration of an interior design project can vary depending on the scope and complexity of the project. However, on average, our projects take between 4-8 weeks to complete. Our team works diligently to meet deadlines and ensure timely project delivery.
