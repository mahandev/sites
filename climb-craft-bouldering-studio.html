Our Story

Climb Craft Bouldering Studio
Pune

At Climb Craft Bouldering Studio, we're a community of passionate climbers and experienced coaches, working together to provide a supportive and challenging environment for climbers of all levels. With years of collective experience, we've crafted a space that's both welcoming and intimidating – the perfect blend for those looking to push their limits and reach new heights.

Our customers rave about our helpful staff, clean facilities, and diverse range of climbing routes. Join the Climb Craft community today and discover a supportive and inspiring environment that will take your climbing to the next level.

FAQ

Got Questions?

What are your opening hours?

We're open from 12:30 pm to 9:30 pm, Monday to Sunday.

Do I need to book in advance?

While not required, booking in advance is recommended to avoid long wait times.

What should I wear?

Dress in comfortable clothing and shoes suitable for climbing. We recommend removing any jewelry and securing long hair.

Can I bring friends who don't climb?

Absolutely! We have a relaxing lounge area where non-climbing friends can socialize and wait while you climb.
